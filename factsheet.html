Objective

The primary objective of the MAHDA Crypto Fund is to deliver long term capital growth at moderate levels of volatility relative to the crypto asset class. MAHDA Crypto Fund provides investors with exposure to the Bitcoin by market capitalization, while providing downside risk protection via a cash hedge during times of market stress. The fund is suitable for medium- to long-term investors seeking healthy levels of exposure to the Bitcoin.

Fund Strategy

Using statistical modeling and machine learning, various quantitative trading models have been developed that use market momentum, trend, volume and hundreds of signal characteristics to take the market characteristics. This process indicates the MAHDA Crypto Fund position toward market behavior for the next time horizon. MAHDA Crypto Fund considers market price movement during different time horizon and extract above 500 numerical features represent different market views varieties, such as trends, volatility, volume, momentum, etc.
